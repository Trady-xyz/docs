# Getting Started

Setting up your account on the platform only takes a few seconds. Follow these steps to begin your trading journey.

## Quick start guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with email, social login, or wallet connection. [Learn more →](/trady/create-account)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit funds">
    Add crypto or buy with a card to fund your account. [Learn more →](/trady/deposit)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    You're ready to make your first trade on Trady
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need help?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I connect my wallet?">
    Navigate to your profile settings and select wallet connection. You can connect both EVM and Solana wallets. [Learn more →](/trady/create-account)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What deposit methods are available?">
    You can deposit crypto from your wallet or buy directly with a card via Moonpay. [Learn more →](/trady/deposit)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I secure my account?">
    Enable 2FA, set up an anti-phishing code, and configure withdrawal limits in Security Settings. [Learn more →](/trady/security-settings)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
