# Withdraw

You can withdraw your funds from Trady to any external wallet address. Your funds will arrive soon after confirmation.

## How to withdraw

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an asset">
    Choose the cryptocurrency you want to withdraw
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter recipient address">
    Provide the wallet address where you want to receive the funds
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the network">
    Select the blockchain network for the withdrawal
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the amount">
    Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and review the transaction details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete withdrawal">
    Confirm the transaction and wait for processing
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important considerations

<Warning>
Double-check the recipient address and network before confirming. Sending to the wrong address or network may result in permanent loss of funds.
</Warning>

<Info>
Withdrawal processing times vary by network. Most withdrawals are completed within minutes, but some networks may take longer.
</Info>

## Withdrawal limits

Your withdrawal limits depend on your account verification level and security settings. You can view and adjust your limits in the [Security Settings](/trady/security-settings).
