# Deposit

You can deposit funds to your Trady account using multiple methods to start trading quickly.

## Deposit methods

### Crypto deposit

Transfer cryptocurrency from your existing wallet:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a network">
    Select the blockchain network you want to use for the deposit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your deposit address">
    Copy the address or scan the QR code provided
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send funds">
    Transfer funds from your wallet to the provided address
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If your wallet is already linked to your Trady account, you can deposit in one click.
</Note>

### Buy crypto with card

Purchase cryptocurrency directly using your credit or debit card:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select buy with card">
    Choose the card payment option
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete purchase via Moonpay">
    Follow the Moonpay flow to complete your purchase
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive funds">
    Your crypto will be deposited directly to your account
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important notes

<Warning>
Always verify the network and address before sending funds. Sending to the wrong network may result in permanent loss of funds.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Linking your wallet enables one-click deposits for faster transactions.
</Tip>
