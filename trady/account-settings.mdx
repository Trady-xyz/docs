# Account Settings

My Profile is the central place for managing your account, assets, security settings, and activity history.

## Overview

Your account overview displays key information at a glance:

- **Wallets**: Connected EVM and Solana wallets
- **Trading volume**: Your total trading activity
- **User tier**: Your current account level
- **Total balance**: Combined value of all assets
- **Recent transactions**: Latest account activity

Quick actions like deposit and withdrawal are available directly from the overview.

## Assets

View a full breakdown of all your assets:

- **Balances**: Current holdings for each asset
- **Value**: USD equivalent of your holdings
- **Price movement**: Recent price changes

Each asset includes quick action buttons:

## Orders

Track all your trading activity:

- **Open orders**: Currently active orders waiting for execution
- **Completed orders**: Historical order records
- **Order status**: Real-time execution updates
- **Cancel orders**: Cancel any active order at any time

You can review execution details and manage orders directly from this section.

## Transactions

A detailed log of every action you've made:

- **Deposits**: Incoming transfers to your account
- **Withdrawals**: Outgoing transfers from your account
- **Trades**: All executed trades
- **Date organization**: Chronologically sorted activity
- **Full status info**: Complete transaction details

Use this section to track your complete account history and verify all transactions.
