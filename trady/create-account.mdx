# Create Account

To create your account, sign up with email or log in instantly via Twitter, Discord, or other social accounts. Wallet registration is also supported.

## Sign up options

You can create your account using any of the following methods:

- **Email**: Sign up with your email address
- **Social accounts**: Log in instantly via Twitter, Discord, or other platforms
- **Wallet**: Register directly with your crypto wallet

## Customize your profile

After creating your account, you can personalize it:

1. **Avatar and nickname**: Set up your profile appearance
2. **Leaderboard visibility**: Choose whether to appear on the leaderboard
3. **Email**: Add your email address for notifications
4. **Social accounts**: Link additional social accounts

## Connect your wallets

Connect your EVM and Solana wallets to authorize access and start trading:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to wallet settings">
    Go to your profile and select wallet connection
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select wallet type">
    Choose between EVM (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.) or Solana wallets
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize access">
    Approve the connection request in your wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can connect multiple wallets to your account for maximum flexibility.
</Note>
