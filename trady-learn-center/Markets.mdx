The Markets section is your central hub for fast crypto navigation. Most tokens can be expanded with a single click, opening a dedicated page inside the Trade tab. This makes Markets a real-time discovery tool where you can instantly track activity, analyze performance, and jump into trades without friction.

**The Market** section provides a real-time overview of today’s crypto landscape. It includes key indicators such as:

- Total market cap
- Average market RSI
- Fear & Greed Index
- Altcoin season indicator

All metrics are displayed with interactive charts that make it easy to understand the market mood and dynamics.

Below, you’ll find a full cryptocurrency catalog with price, trading volume, market cap, 24h change, and supported networks. Sections like **Favorites**, **All Crypto**, **Highlights** let you track trends, discover new releases, and monitor rising or declining assets. Clicking on any token opens its detailed trading page, making **Market** the core navigation hub for fast trading.

### Highlights

![Highlights](/images/Highlights-2.png)

**Highlights** is the platform’s analytics hub, collecting the most important market signals in one place. The page automatically ranks assets across key categories: Trending, Gainers, Losers, New, helping traders instantly spot opportunities, strong performers, correction candidates, and newly emerging tokens.

#### Trending

**Trending** shows the tokens with the highest current market activity based on:

- sudden spikes in attention
- high trading volumes
- strong social-signal surges
- rapid price movements

Trending helps you instantly see what the market is talking about _right now_.

#### Gainers

A list of the top-performing assets for the selected time period. Each token displays:

- price change (absolute and %)
- market cap
- quick-action **Buy** button

This section is ideal for spotting assets that are already showing strength and trending upward.

#### Losers

Assets with the strongest declines during the chosen timeframe. Useful for:

- finding dip-entry opportunities
- monitoring high-risk assets
- assessing the depth of corrections

#### New

The newest tokens added to the platform or recently activated contracts. Showing:

- early performance
- first activity signals
- potential opportunities to enter “at the start”

#### Token Card

Each token card displays:

- token name, icon, and network
- market cap and price
- price change for the selected period
- contract address
- quick **Buy** button

### Trenches

![Trenches](/images/Trenches-2.png)

**Trenches** lets you trade meme coins straight from launchpads like [Pump.fun](http://Pump.fun). Every token moves through stages – from launch to migration – and you can track metrics, spot momentum, and enter at any point of the lifecycle.

- **New** _Ground floor entries_. Fresh off the launchpad – tokens that just went live and are starting to build momentum. Track basic metrics (MC, price, TXs) and spot early traction before the crowd catches on.
- **Almost Bonded** _Often the best entry window_. Tokens approaching the bonding threshold. The Progress bar shows exactly how close they are to migration. Enter here to front-run the liquidity shift to DEX.
- **Migrated** _Stable liquidity, lower risk_. Bonding complete. These tokens have graduated to established DEX pools with smoother execution and less volatility. Full lifecycle done – what you see is what you get.

**You can filter your search by:**

- **Launchpads**: Pumpfun, Bonk, Four Meme, Catapult, Meteora, Catapult
- **Quote Token**: USD, SOL, ETH, BNB
- **Metrics (short & full)**: B.Curve, Age, Liquidity, KOLs, Dev migrated, Dev launches, Dev migrated %, Holders, Recent Visitors, Top 10 holdings %, Dev holdings %, Insiders %, Bundlers %, Snipers %, Bot Traders, Global Fees Paid, Volume 24h, Volume, MC, Buys , Sells, TXs
- **Socials**: With at least 1 social, DEX Ad Paid, Dex Boost, CTO, Only Tweet, Dex Bar Paid, Update Social, Website, YouTube, Instagram, X, Telegram, TikTok

### Blacklist

**The Blacklist** is a user-controlled filtering tool that hides unwanted assets, developers, keywords, and sources across the platform. Anything matching your rules disappears from feeds, recommendations, search results, and lists.

Blacklist helps you:

- avoid scam developers
- filter out unwanted memes or communities
- block specific keywords
- hide certain KOLs, websites, or contract addresses

### Hotkeys 

![Hotkeys](/images/Hotkeys-3.png)

Hot keys enable traders to execute orders instantly, reducing the time between decision-making and action. 

Combine the Pause + Modifier + Row keys to buy tokens

**Modifier Keys**

These are keys you hold **in addition to the Pause key** to modify the action. 

**Row Keys**

These are the specific keys corresponding to actions on a row (or token) in your interface. Generally these are numbers or letters representing rows.

| **Token Type** | **Keys to Hold & Press**                    | **Action**              |
| :------------- | :------------------------------------------ | :---------------------- |
| New Pairs      | Space + Shift (or Option) + Row key         | Buy New Pairs token     |
| Final Stretch  | Space + Shift + Ctrl + Option + Row key     | Buy Final Stretch token |
| Migrated       | Space + Shift + Ctrl + Option + H + Row key | Buy Migrated token      |
