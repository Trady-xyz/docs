The **Portfolio** section gives users a complete, real-time overview of their crypto assets and performance. Users can view key metrics, track historical changes, and take quick actions on any asset.

### Account Performance

This section provides a high-level view of your overall portfolio performance:

- **Current Balance** – see the current estimated value of all assets.
- **Total PnL (Profit & Loss)** – track overall gains and losses.
- **Trading Volume** – understand your market activity.
- **Trades & Win Rate** – evaluate trading efficiency.
- **Performance Charts**: Total Assets Value: Visualizes changes in portfolio value over time. Cumulative PnL %: Compare your performance with BTC price movements to evaluate strategy effectiveness relative to the market.
