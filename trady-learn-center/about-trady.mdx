Trady is a decentralized alpha trading terminal that unifies cross-chain trading, analytics, and portfolio management in one seamless,drag-and-drop interface. Built for on-chain traders with a full suite of tools that make trading faster, more efficient, and secure.

**Core Principles:**

- **Unified Balance.** View and manage all your assets across networks in one place.
- **Gas Abstraction.** Trade and interact on-chain without worrying about gas complexity.
- Aggregated Assets. All your tokens, positions, and liquidity, seamlessly aggregated.
- **Non-custodial by design.** You stay in full control of your assets at all times.
- **12+ networks supported.** Trade across leading EVM & Solana ecosystems from a single interface.

### Fees

The platform follows a transparent, predictable fee structure. We only charge trading fees, which depend on your user tier and trading volume; lower fees for active traders.
