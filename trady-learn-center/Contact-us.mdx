## How to Get Help from Trady

If you need assistance or have any questions, you can contact the Trady support team in two ways.

**Trady Discord (Ticket System):**

> 1. Join the official Trady Discord server.
> 2. Open the Support / Tickets channel.
> 3. Create a ticket and describe your issue in detail.
> 4. A support team member will reply directly in your ticket.

Discord is **the primary channel** for technical support and account-related issues.

**Website Support Widget**

You can also contact support directly through the support widget on the Trady website:

> 1. Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the site.
> 2. Describe your question or issue.
> 3. Submit the message — the support team will get back to you as soon as possible.

Recommendation:

 Use Discord for urgent or technical issues.

 Use the website support widget for general questions.
